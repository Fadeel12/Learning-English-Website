What is Tenses?

Tenses are grammatical system used to express the time and aspect of an education or event, In English, tenses are crucial because they help clarify when something happens, whether in the past, present, or future. A complex explanation of tenses involves both the time (tense) and the aspect of the verb.

What is TOEIC?

Toeic (Test of English for International Communication) is a standarized English test oriented towards professionals and companies. The TOEIC is actually two separate tests, one which tests the receptive English skills and one which tests the productive English skills.
